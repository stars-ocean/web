Magnetohydrodynamic Simulation of Plasma-Magnetosphere Interaction

Solar wind is a stream of plasma (electrically conducting fluid). For my master thesis, I performed the simulation of the interaction between the plasma and the Earth's magnetosphere using the magnetohydrodynamics (MHD), which can be considered as the traditional computational fluid dynamics coupling with Maxwell's euqations. Few days ago, I occasionally found a movie I used for my defense, quite interesting, so I put it here. The movie is a visualization of a simulation of a real space weather event: Halloween solar storm in 2003.
